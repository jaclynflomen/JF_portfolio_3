Photography

From light to dark, the contrast shown when white and black near each other allows for the emphasis on various elements. In my introduction to the creative industry, I explored monochromatic photography to help establich a base knowledge of contrast, emphasis, and how to draw the viewer's eye to convey a message.
The content, while varying, allowed me to learn how to create a rounded composition and establish an identity through my work.
