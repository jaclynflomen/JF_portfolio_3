Ontario Tourism Campaign

ONTARIO SUMMER - Find Yourself At Home


Problem

Given 24 hours, create a campaign for a tourism company, including an application with a video element, back-end development, and an overall identity.

Solution

As the Project Manager for this venture, I took on a leadership role, ensuring everything get done on time and to the group's best ability. When first recieving this assignment, my group wanted to establish an identity fairly quickly, as the rest of the campaign relied on having a set brand.
While the graphic designer took on his role in creating the look of the campaign, I aided the front-end and back-end developers in establishing a base for the campaign to be displayed. Through communication, teamwork, and perseverence, we created the media project, following all specifications and including our own sense of self to the work.
This hands-on experience taught me the importance of time-management and gave me the opportunity to see how a real-world venture would be produced.
